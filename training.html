Quick Tips

Proper Form

Always prioritize proper form over weight. This prevents injuries and ensures effective muscle engagement.

Progressive Overload

Gradually increase weight or reps to continue making progress and building strength.

Rest & Recovery

Allow adequate rest between sets and workouts. Muscles grow during recovery, not during exercise.

Warm-up

Always warm up properly before lifting weights to prepare your muscles and prevent injuries.
